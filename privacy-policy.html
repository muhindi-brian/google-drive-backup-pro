Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

DriveBackup Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Google Drive Backup Tool application and related services.

This policy applies to all users of our application, including those who access our services through our website, desktop application, or any other means.

2. Google User Data Access and Usage

ðŸ”’ Important: Google User Data Access

DriveBackup Pro accesses your Google Drive data solely for the purpose of creating local backups of your files. We do not store, analyze, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

2.1 Google OAuth 2.0 Authentication

  • Authentication Process: When you use DriveBackup Pro, you authenticate with Google using OAuth 2.0. This process is handled entirely by Google's secure authentication system.
  • Access Tokens: We receive temporary access tokens from Google to access your Drive files. These tokens are stored locally on your device and encrypted.
  • No Credentials Stored: We never store your Google account username, password, or any other login credentials.
  • Token Management: Access tokens are automatically refreshed by Google's OAuth system and stored securely on your local device.

2.2 Google Drive Data Access

  • Read-Only Access: DriveBackup Pro requests read-only access to your Google Drive files and folders. We cannot modify, delete, or create files in your Drive.
  • File Metadata Access: We access file names, sizes, modification dates, MIME types, and folder structure to facilitate backup operations.
  • File Content Access: We download the actual content of your files to create local backups. This is the core functionality of our application.
  • Shared Drive Access: If you have access to shared drives, we can access files in those drives that you have permission to view.
  • Scope of Access: We only access files and folders that you explicitly select for backup. We do not scan or access your entire Drive without your consent.

2.3 Data Processing and Storage

  • Local Processing: All file processing occurs locally on your device. Files are downloaded directly to your computer.
  • No Server Storage: We do not upload or store any of your files on our servers. All backup data remains on your local device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not use any cloud storage services to store your backup data.
  • No Data Analysis: We do not analyze the content of your files for any purpose other than creating backups.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share your files or data with any third parties.

2.4 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Application Settings: We store your application preferences and configuration settings locally on your device.
  • Backup Logs: We maintain logs of backup operations locally for troubleshooting purposes.
  • Error Reports: We may collect anonymous error reports to improve application stability (no personal data included).
  • Usage Statistics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our application (no personal or file data included).

2.5 Support Communications

  • Support Requests: If you contact us for support, we may collect information you provide in those communications.
  • No File Data in Support: We do not request or collect your actual files or personal data in support communications.
  • Support Data Retention: Support communications are retained for service improvement and may be stored securely.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To authenticate with Google Drive and perform backup operations as requested.
  • Improve Application: To analyze usage patterns and improve our application functionality.
  • Technical Support: To provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your backup files are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your files on our servers.

4.2 Authentication Tokens

Google OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device in encrypted format. These tokens are used only to authenticate with Google Drive APIs.

4.3 Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption for sensitive data.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted.
  • We implement secure coding practices to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Google APIs: We share authentication tokens with Google to access your Drive files as authorized by you.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our application (e.g., hosting providers, analytics services).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Control: You can access, modify, or delete your local application data at any time.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke our access to your Google Drive through your Google Account settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of anonymous usage data collection in the application settings.
  • Data Portability: You can export your application settings and configuration data.
  • Deletion: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the application.

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Your backup files and application data are stored locally and retained until you delete them or uninstall the application.
  • Authentication Tokens: Google OAuth tokens are stored locally and automatically refreshed by Google. You can revoke access at any time.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 2 years for analysis and improvement purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is processed and stored locally on your device. However, when you use Google Drive APIs, your data may be transferred to Google's servers, which may be located in different countries. We rely on Google's compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Displaying a notification in the application

Your continued use of our application after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
