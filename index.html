Data Privacy & Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about how we handle your data

What Data We Access

DriveBackup Pro requests read-only access to your Google Drive files, folders, and metadata. We access this data solely to create local backups of your content.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for backup purposes. We do not store, analyze, share, or use your files for any other purpose. All processing happens locally on your computer.

Data Security

We use OAuth2 authentication for secure access. Your files are downloaded directly to your local machine - we never store them on our servers or transmit them to third parties.

Privacy Policy

Our comprehensive privacy policy details exactly how we handle your data. We encourage you to review it to understand your rights and our commitments.
