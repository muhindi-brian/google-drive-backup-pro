System Requirements

Memory

4GB RAM minimum, 8GB recommended for optimal performance with large backup operations.

Storage

Network

Stable broadband internet connection required for downloading files from Google Drive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about downloading and installing

Is the download free?

Yes, DriveBackup Pro is completely free to download and use. We believe in making powerful backup tools accessible to everyone.

No installation required! The executable file is self-contained and includes all necessary dependencies. Just extract and run.

Absolutely! Our software is open source, regularly scanned for malware, and uses secure OAuth2 authentication. Your data never leaves your control.
